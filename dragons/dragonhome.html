DRAGONS

Be it Puff, Smaug, Smrgol, Gorbash, Draco, Dexter, Falkor, King Ghidorah, Haku, Mushu, Thoron, Targon, Aroara, Ferrarius, Argentum, Gryndel, Irascor, Slann, Saphhira, Norberta, Yowler, or countless others, dragons are far and away some of the coolest creatures around.


Something has always fascinated me about Dragons. These large winged beasts are one of the most commonly used magical creatures in literature. Almost every single fantasy novel that includes magic and monsters, will have at least a reference to these majestic creatures.
